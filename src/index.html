About

Ground . Release . Change . Connect

Our aim is to support, educate, and nurture you increasing your awareness of who you are in each given moment without judgement or shame. Alignment with this presence takes practice and an ability to really see you, past any unconscious conditioned pattern, habit, belief, thought, etc., which are held within body. This holding can cause physical, emotional and spiritual pain and halt any personal growth in all areas of your life.

We support those who are interested in developing and listening to the deeper meaning and reality of who you are beyond your ego, neuronal firing, family or cultural conditioning, personality, anxiety, thoughts, beliefs, etc. Using a multitude of tools I’ll help you explore and discover a more integrated way of being. The real learning is uncovering the connection with the self while you stay in connection with others and name the temporary conditioning thoughts patterns and beliefs. It’a a practicing in the back and forth synchronicity between illusion and reality.

Waking up isn’t mystical, everyone can do it. You have to be willing to recognize, look at and move through the illusion you’ve created and see the reality that’s always here and available to you and focus your attention there. It takes time and commitment. This exploring is through experience individually and collectively in witnessing yourself and others break free of their trauma, stress, conditioned patterns, holding, etc. It’s an essential part of the real education and learning.

Whether you are looking for therapeutic bodywork or if you are sincerely interested in exploring the deeper aspects of the self, we can help you relax, explore and discover your deeper reality and understanding of who you are. We do not push ideals, methods, religions, beliefs, or concepts.

Ildikó combines her clinically based techniques, intuition, and presence to create an individual experience for each client. Services

Integrative Release

Beginner Integrative Release

Trauma affects the body on many different levels. Some people don’t even realize how much they're storing/holding within their whole system. Using Awakening to Presence (ATP) practices you learn to bring awareness within the parts of the whole system, letting go and allowing any conditioned patterns to release, and realigning with truth. Sessions are done with loose clothing, includes 15 minute check in, and may include bodywork, movement, cognitive understanding, among other methods chosen by the practitioner to help educate and facilitate release of body contraction.

90 minutes - $125

Advanced Integrative Release

Once you have learned to effectively begin releasing parts of the conditioned patterning in Beginner Integrative Release, you will explore this truth moving more deeply into the true nature of your existence. Allowing patterns to show up as they are requires safety, trust and willingness to go deeper. Exploring the unconscious patterns that keep repeating, the patterns we get hung up on the most and keep us from our deepest desires of true connection are challenging to explore alone. Sessions include check in, cognitive and felt sense exploration, and recommended group work.

60 minutes - $100

John F Barnes Myofascial

Myofascial massage is done with clothes on, shorts and a sports top for women. The therapist will statically hold where the fascia is restricted for long periods of time in order to facilitate its release while encouraging the client to bring awareness and presence to any sensations in their body.

60 minutes - $85
90 minutes - $125

Deep Feet Ashiatsu

Ashi = Foot, Atsu = Pressure. Using barefoot massage techniques (foot compressions and gliding) to deliver a deep tissue massage, the practitioner uses overhead bars for support. The foot is broad and makes compression smooth therefore not painful. Pressure can be adjusted at a level that's comfortable for the client. It provides deep relaxation, in addition to releasing adhesions, held tensions, and toxins at a more rapid rate compared to traditional massage techniques.

60 minutes - $85
90 minutes - $125
