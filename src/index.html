About

Welcome to The Touch Of Presence - A sanctuary of support, education, and nurturing for your innermost self.

Ground . Release . Change . Connect

Our Mission At The Touch Of Presence, our mission is to empower, educate, and nurture individuals on their journey towards self-awareness and self-acceptance. We strive to help you tap into the deepest parts of yourself—those aspects that are inherently authentic, unwavering, and whole, without judgment or shame.

Embracing Authenticity Embarking on this journey means delving deep within your patterning consciously and more crucially seeing the unconscious conditioning within patterns, habits, beliefs, and perceptions that reside within your being. Such internalized holding can inflict physical, emotional, and spiritual distress, hindering personal growth and leaving you ensnared in repetitive patterns, feelings of frustration, anger, or loneliness. You might find yourself donning a facade or resorting to substances to numb the pain.

Our Approach Our approach revolves around the practice of bringing awareness and consciousness to conditioned patterns, habits, and beliefs that reside within your being. These ingrained patterns can manifest as physical, emotional, and spiritual pain, hindering personal growth and leaving you feeling stuck, frustrated, and disconnected. Through our guidance and support, we aim to empower you to navigate beyond these limitations, fostering and inspiring a deeper understanding of reality that transcends ego, societal conditioning, and fear.

Tools for Self-Exploration We offer a diverse range of tools and techniques designed to facilitate self-exploration and discovery. Whether through individual sessions or group settings, we encourage you to delve into the depths of your consciousness, exploring the interconnectedness between self and environment. By naming and acknowledging conditioning, thought patterns, and beliefs, you can embark on a transformative journey towards self-realization and authenticity.

Embracing Vulnerability Waking up to your true nature is not an esoteric concept reserved for a select few—it is a journey accessible to all who are willing to confront their illusions and redirect their attention inward. This process requires time, commitment, and a willingness to embrace vulnerability. Through our collaborative efforts, we invite you to break free from the constraints of trauma, stress, and conditioned patterns, and embark on a path of self-discovery and liberation.

Uncovering and Transforming At our core, our practice is about uncovering and transforming, beyond conditioning. A journey for all, connecting with your inner depths, and sustaining presence beyond what you could dream of experiencing. We encourage you to stand unabashedly in your authenticity, upholding integrity, and facing both your conscious and unconscious tendencies without justification or evasion.

Personalized Growth Journey Whether you seek therapeutic bodywork or are eager to explore the deeper dimensions of self, we are here to support you every step of the way. We do not impose ideologies, methods, or beliefs onto our clients; rather, we empower you to take ownership of your growth journey at your own pace. Our approach includes guiding individuals to recognize unconscious behaviors, even when confronting the challenging aspects of oneself may not be easy. We provide direct, empathetic support, fostering an environment where you feel encouraged to confront and work through these areas.

Embrace Self-Discovery Connecting with the deeper aspects of yourself is akin to diving into the still depths of the ocean amidst turbulent waves. The stillness that resides within you remains unwavering, beckoning you to embrace it with open arms. At The Touch Of Presence, we invite you to embark on a journey of self-discovery, where the depths of your truth await your exploration.

Our Specialized Techniques Swedish
A traditional Swedish massage uses light, medium, or firm pressure to promote relaxation, stimulate nerve endings, and increase blood flow and lymph drainage. This gentle technique is ideal for those seeking relaxation and tension relief. If you need help with an injury or require deeper work, our Deep Tissue/Ashiatsu massage may be more suited to your needs.

Ashiatsu Therapy
Ashiatsu (Ashi = Foot, Atsu = Pressure) uses foot compressions with overhead bars for support. The broad foot compressions provide smooth, deep pressure that releases adhesions, tension, and toxins more rapidly than traditional techniques. Each session includes a consultation to understand your specific needs.

Myofascial Release
Myofascial release involves wearing shorts and a sports top (for women) while the therapist holds areas of fascia restriction for extended periods, facilitating release. This technique encourages clients to bring awareness and presence to sensations in their bodies. Each session includes a consultation to tailor the treatment to your goals.

Neuronal Integration Release/Bodywork
This technique uses somatic releasing methods to address the automatic feedback loop between mind and body. By engaging and releasing this loop, clients gain more flexibility in their actions and decisions, reducing rigidity in thoughts and perceptions. This can lead to greater freedom, less chronic pain, and improved mental well-being over time. Each session includes a consultation to address your specific needs and goals.

What to Expect

Preparing for Your First Appointment

• Arrive Early: Give yourself plenty of time to locate our facility. We are committed to starting appointments promptly.

• Complete Paperwork: Ensure all required paperwork is completed online before your appointment. We cannot accommodate paperwork completion at the time of your visit, and failure to do so may result in rescheduling and potential charges.

• Appointment Start: Your appointment begins when your provider escorts you to the treatment room. They will review your initial form, address any questions, and discuss your immediate concerns.

During Your Appointment

• Preparation: Your therapist will leave the room to prepare for your session, ensuring you are comfortable on the table.

• Cell Phone Policy: Turn off your cell phone to focus on your relaxation.

• Post-Session Care: Allow yourself time to relax after your session. Avoid strenuous activities or returning immediately to work.

Massage Information

• Purpose: Massage aims to relax your body and shift your nervous system into a parasympathetic state. Any touch involved is strictly professional and non-sexual.

• Uncomfortable Situations: If the therapist feels uncomfortable at any point, they will leave the room, and you will be charged for the full duration of the service.

• Paperwork: Complete all necessary paperwork online prior to your appointment. Failure to do so may result in a shortened session.

• Personal Hygiene: Arrive fresh and clean. You may want to exfoliate your skin beforehand.

• Illness: Do not come to your session if you are feeling unwell.

• Hydration: Drink water before and after your appointment to aid in detoxification and hydration.

• Communication: Clearly communicate your goals and boundaries with your therapist. Undress to your comfort level, typically with less clothing to facilitate treatment. Expect to be draped during the massage.

• Emotional Release: Emotional or somatic release can occur during a session. If you feel uncomfortable, communicate with your therapist immediately.

Neuronal Integration Bodywork Release Sessions

• Clothing: Wear loose-fitting clothing such as a t-shirt and shorts or sweatpants. Bring these items to change into after your initial assessment.

• Personal Hygiene: Arrive fresh and clean.

• Illness: Do not attend your session if you are feeling unwell.

• Emotional and Physical Release: Be prepared for potential emotional and physical releases. Communication with your therapist is crucial.

• Silence and Listening: Silence is important for tuning into your body's signals. Follow your practitioner's instructions to align with your best interests.

• Post-Session Care: After your session, take time to process your experience. Focus on relaxation and reach out to your practitioner or a mental health professional if needed.

Change and Cancellation Policy

• Advance Notice: Cancellations should be made 48 hours or more in advance. Late cancellations (less than 48 hours) incur a fee of 50% of the appointment value. Same-day cancellations or no-shows will be charged 100% of the appointment value.

• Pre-Payments: Clients who cancel less than 48 hours in advance twice will be required to pre-pay for appointments, which will be non-refundable.

Running Late

• Late Arrivals: Arriving late may result in a shortened session or rescheduling, with a "Missed Appointment Fee" applied.

COVID-19 Response

• Safety Protocols: We follow guidelines from the CDC, the Virginia Department of Health, and the American Massage Therapy Association.

• Masks: Optional for both clients and therapists.

• Vaccination and Testing: Our therapists are vaccinated or have natural immunity. We adhere to CDC guidelines for testing and isolation.

• Sanitization: All surfaces are cleaned and sanitized before and after each session. Air purifiers are used in treatment rooms.

• Symptoms: If you exhibit any COVID-19 symptoms, reschedule your appointment.

Payment Information

• Credit Card Requirement: A credit card is required to hold all appointments, enforcing our cancellation policy.

• Accepted Payments: Currently, we accept cash, checks, Venmo, and gift certificates. Future plans include accepting credit cards.

• Gratuity: Tips can be added to gift certificates, but direct tipping via cash or Venmo is preferred.

Tipping Guidance

• Gratuity Amount: Tips are appreciated and can range from 10% to 20% of the service amount, based on the quality of the service and your personal means.

We look forward to providing you with a relaxing and rejuvenating experience. Please feel free to reach out with any questions or concerns.
